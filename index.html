Why Udine?

From Renaissance architecture to delicious food and wine, Udine is a great place to live.

Located in the heart of the charming Friuli-Venezia Giulia region in Italy's far northeast, Udine is a picturesque and authentic city with striking architecture that reflects over 350 years of Venetian rule. This small region, nestled between the Alps and the Adriatic Sea and bordering Slovenia and Austria, is renowned for its exceptional wine production. Udine's numerous historic wine bars are perfect spots to savor the excellent local wines.
